  
An **embedded system** is a computer built into a larger machine to provide a means of control.  
  
They perform a **very specific, pre-programmed task**, which is stored in **ROM**.  
  
Using a combination of **hardware** and **software**, embedded systems carry out these tasks as efficiently as possible, often with **limited storage** and **processing power**.
